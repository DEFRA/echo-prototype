Apply for an export health certificate

To apply online you'll need:

You can buy:

  • personal or company contact details
  • the name and address of your official veterinarian
  • information about the destination

It is free to use this service but you will need to pay for your vet's services

Start now

Using the service for the first time
The first time you use the service you'll need a Government Gateway and Defra identity account. If you do not have these accounts you'll be prompted to create them.

If you work for a business or organisation you'll need to create an account that's linked to your business contact details. You need your email address, phone number and postal address.

If you are registering on behalf of a business or organisation, you wil also need the:

  • main contact details for your business or organisation (for example, the head office)
  • Companies House number if you're registering on behalf of a limited company

Sign in when you have a Defra account
You can access your Defra account (including export health certificates) from the Defra account management page.
