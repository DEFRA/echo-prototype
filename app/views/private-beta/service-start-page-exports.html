Apply for an export health certificate

Thank you for agreeing to take part in the early access testing of the new online Export Health Certificate service.

You'll be able to use this service to manage your applications for EHCs online including cloning existing applications.

To access the service, you will need:

  • the user name and password you were sent by email
  • your Government Gateway user ID and password (if you don't already have an account, you will be guided to create one)
  • a Defra identity account (again, if you don't have one set up, you'll be guided through that process)

Setting up a Government Gateway ID and Defra identity

Make sure you associate these accounts with your organisation, not an individual. You will need:

  • your work email address, phone number and postal address
  • the main contact details for your business or organisation (for example, head office contact details)
  • the Companies House number if you are registering on behalf of a limited company

APHA have to process your Defra identity registration, so please allow time for that to happen.
