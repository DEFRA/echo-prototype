Export sheepskins to China: certificate 5725

Apply for an export health certificate to export sheepskins to the People’s Republic of China.

Apply for an export health certificate online

Exporters in England, Scotland and Wales can apply for an export health certificate (EHC) online.

If you’re in Northern Ireland, you’ll need to apply to DAERA

Applying online means you can:

  • see the status of your applications
  • always use the latest version of the EHC
  • copy existing applications
  • apply for blocks of certificates

Checks you need for your export

Find out about requirements for export [PDF]

Apply online

You’ll need:

  • a Government Gateway account
  • a Defra IDC

You will need to create these accounts if you do not already have them.

Your official vet or inspector will need to register to use the service.

Start now

Find out how to register to use this service [PDF].

Other ways to apply

You can still apply by sending your application to APHA by email or uploading it for DAERA in Northern Ireland.

Make sure you use the latest version of the EHC as they are sometimes updated. The latest version in on this page.

If you do not use the latest version, APHA or DAERA will not accept your application.

Exporters in Northern Ireland, upload your application. Check if there are any other forms to submit with your application in the commodity section of the DAERA website.

Help with your application

In England, Scotland and Wales, call APHA on 03000 200 301.

In Northern Ireland, call DAERA on 02877442060.

Find out about call charges.

Apply by email guidance

The following documents have guidance, extra export conditions and information.

The guidance notes are split into parts, to help you with each part of the certificate.

  • Guidance notes for certificate 6969

What you need to do

You need to fill out this certificate, the export application form (EXA) and all other documents in Section A to export. The guidance notes in Section B have information for exporters and official veterinarians.

Email all documents in Section A to processingteam@apha.gov.uk. Send one email for each application.
