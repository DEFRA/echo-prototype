Option 2: Apply by email

Download, fill out and email.

  • Export Application Form (ET169)

  • Export raw milk, dairy products, colostrum and colostrum-based products intended for transit through or storage in the EU: certificate 8283, version 3.

Apply by email guidance

The following documents have guidance, extra export conditions and information.

The guidance notes are split into parts, to help you with each part of the certificate.

  • Guidance notes for certificate 8283, version 3.

What you need to do

You need to fill out this certificate, the export application form (EXA) and all other documents in Section A to export. The guidance notes in Section B have information for exporters and official veterinarians.

Email all documents in Section A to processingteam@apha.gov.uk. Send one email for each application.

What exporters in Northern Ireland need to do

  • Find documents and instructions for exporting pig meat to China from Northern Ireland.

Always use the latest certificate

Before you apply, make sure you have the most up to date version of the certificate as certificates are sometimes updated. The most up to date versions will be on this page.

If you do not use the latest version, your application will not be accepted.
