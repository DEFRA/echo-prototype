In cases where two UK ports are involved prior to the consignment leaving the UK, then both ports are to be entered in the certificate alongside the respective dates of sailing. e.g. Belfast (23/03/2018) / Southampton (29/03/2018)
Where two ports are involved on arrival to China, i.e. transhipment at initial port of arrival in China for internal travel to the final port of entry where the final import clearance will be carried out, then both ports need to be entered
In the majority of cases the means of transport should be entered as ‘Refrigerated Container by Sea’. The name of the ship need not be entered in this section.
